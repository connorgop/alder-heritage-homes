## Part 8: Google Ads — Small Budget Strategy

With a small budget, the goal is **maximum Quality Score** (which lowers your cost per click) and **zero wasted spend**.

### Campaign Structure

Run **3 separate campaigns**, each targeting a different intent level:

**Campaign 1: Distress Keywords (Highest Intent — Spend Most Here)**
- Budget: 60% of total
- Keywords: "can't afford mortgage Fresno", "behind on mortgage help Fresno", "stop foreclosure Fresno", "ARM loan adjusting Fresno"
- Landing pages: /lp/cant-afford-home, /lp/stop-foreclosure, /lp/arm-rate-change, /lp/behind-on-mortgage
- Expected CPC: $12–$25

**Campaign 2: Cash Buyer Keywords (Medium Intent)**
- Budget: 30% of total
- Keywords: "sell house fast Fresno", "cash home buyers Fresno", "we buy houses Fresno"
- Landing pages: /lp/sell-house-fast, homepage
- Expected CPC: $25–$60

**Campaign 3: Anti-Wholesaler (Low Cost, High Differentiation)**
- Budget: 10% of total
- Keywords: "wholesaler real estate Fresno", "is cash buyer a wholesaler", "real cash buyer not wholesaler"
- Landing page: /lp/real-cash-buyer-not-wholesaler
- Expected CPC: $5–$15 (nobody is bidding on these)

### Negative Keywords (Add These Immediately)
These prevent your ads from showing for irrelevant searches and save significant budget:
- "jobs", "career", "hiring", "wholesaling course", "how to wholesale", "wholesale real estate training", "investor training", "MLS", "Zillow", "Realtor.com", "rent", "apartment", "rental", "commercial", "land"

# Google Ads Retargeting Campaign Setup Guide

## Overview

Retargeting shows ads to people who visited your website but didn't convert. These visitors are 3-5x more likely to convert than cold traffic because they already know about your business.

**Expected Results:**
- Conversion rate: 8-15% (vs 5-10% for cold traffic)
- Cost per conversion: $15-30 (vs $30-50 for cold traffic)
- ROI: 3-5x better than new visitor campaigns

## Step 5: How to Set Up in Google Ads

### Create Audiences:

1. **Go to:** Google Ads > Tools > Audiences
2. **Click:** "Create audience"
3. **Select:** "Website visitors"
4. **Configure:**
   - **Audience name:** "All Website Visitors - 30 days"
   - **Membership duration:** 30 days
   - **Website visitors:** All visitors

5. **Repeat** for:
   - "Lead Capture Page Visitors - 7 days" (URL contains: /ads/lead-capture)
   - "Blog Readers - 14 days" (URL contains: /blog)

### Create Campaigns:

1. **Go to:** Google Ads > Campaigns > New Campaign
2. **Campaign type:** Search
3. **Campaign goal:** Leads
4. **Campaign name:** "Highest Paying $ - Retargeting"
5. **Locations:** Fresno, Clovis, Visalia, Tulare, Hanford, Bakersfield

## Step 6: Monitoring & Optimization

### Weekly Metrics to Track:

| Metric | Target | Action if Below |
|--------|--------|-----------------|
| Impressions | 500-1,000 | Increase bid or budget |
| Clicks | 50-100 | Improve ad copy |
| Conversions | 5-15 | Optimize landing page |
| Cost per conversion | $15-30 | Adjust bid strategy |
| Conversion rate | 8-15% | Test new ad copy |

### Monthly Optimization:

1. **Pause underperforming ads** (CTR < 2%)
2. **Scale winning ads** (CTR > 3%, CPA < $25)
3. **Test new audiences** (add custom intent audiences)
4. **Refine landing page** (A/B test form fields)
5. **Adjust bids** based on performance

## Key Success Factors

1. **Audience size matters** - Need at least 100-500 people in audience for good results
2. **Bid strategy is critical** - Target CPA of $20-30 for retargeting (lower than new traffic)
3. **Ad copy variation** - Test 3-5 different ad variations per campaign
4. **Landing page optimization** - Make sure /ads/lead-capture is converting
5. **Frequency capping** - Don't show ads more than 3-5 times per day (avoid ad fatigue)

---

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

❌ **Mistake 1:** Using same ad copy for retargeting as new traffic
- **Fix:** Use urgency, social proof, and objection-handling copy for retargeting

❌ **Mistake 2:** Setting bid too low for retargeting
- **Fix:** Retargeting should have higher CPA bid than new traffic (they're more likely to convert)

❌ **Mistake 3:** Not segmenting audiences
- **Fix:** Create separate audiences for different visitor types (lead capture vs blog readers)

❌ **Mistake 4:** Targeting too broad
- **Fix:** Use 7-30 day windows, not 90+ days (older visitors are less likely to convert)

❌ **Mistake 5:** Ignoring frequency
- **Fix:** Cap frequency at 3-5 impressions per day to avoid ad fatigue

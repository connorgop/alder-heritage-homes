## Tips for Better GBP Posts
- **Add a photo every time** — posts with photos get significantly more views. Use a house photo, your headshot, or a before/after photo.
- **Post on the same day each week** — Tuesday or Wednesday mornings tend to perform well.
- **Respond to all reviews within 24 hours** — Google rewards engagement.
- **Use the "Offer" post type** for promotions (e.g., "Free cash offer this week — no obligation").
- **Track which posts drive calls** using UTM parameters on your links (e.g., `?utm_source=gbp&utm_medium=post&utm_campaign=stockton`).

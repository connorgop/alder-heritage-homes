## Quality Score Optimization

### To Improve Quality Score (Goal: 7+/10):

1. **Ad Relevance**
   - Match ad copy to landing page content
   - Use keyword in headline when possible
   - Highlight unique value proposition

2. **Landing Page Experience**
   - Fast load time (< 3 seconds)
   - Mobile-friendly design
   - Clear CTA above the fold
   - Relevant content (no misleading claims)

3. **Click-Through Rate**
   - Test headline variations
   - Use emotional triggers ("WARNING," "EXPOSED")
   - Include social proof (700+ homes)
   - Use specific numbers (3 days, $0 fees)

## Monitoring & Optimization Schedule

### Daily (First 2 Weeks)
- Check impressions (should be 500-1,000/day)
- Check CTR (should be 4-6%)
- Check CPC (should be $3-6)
- Pause underperforming keywords (CTR < 2%)

### Weekly
- Review conversion rate by ad group
- Pause low-performing ads (CTR < 3%)
- Increase bids on high-performing keywords
- Test new ad copy variations
- Review landing page bounce rate

### Bi-Weekly
- Analyze cost per lead by keyword
- Identify top-performing keywords
- Scale budget to winners
- Reduce budget to underperformers
- Test new keywords

### Monthly
- Full campaign performance review
- Calculate ROI by ad group
- Adjust bids and budgets
- Plan next month's strategy
- Report to stakeholder

---

## Red Flags to Watch

1. **High Impressions, Low CTR** → Ad copy isn't compelling
2. **High CTR, Low Conversions** → Landing page isn't converting
3. **High CPC, Low Conversions** → Keyword isn't relevant
4. **Increasing CPC Over Time** → Increased competition (scale budget or pause)
5. **Decreasing CTR Over Time** → Ad fatigue (refresh ad copy)

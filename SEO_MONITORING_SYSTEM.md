## Key Metrics to Track

### 1. Keyword Rankings
- **What:** Position of each keyword in Google search results
- **Where:** Google Search Console (Performance report)
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Target:** Top 10 for main keywords

**Target Keywords:**
- "cash buyer fresno" - Position target: Top 5
- "how to spot a wholesaler" - Position target: Top 10
- "probate home sale fresno" - Position target: Top 10
- "cash buyer vs listing" - Position target: Top 20
- "fresno home prices" - Position target: Top 10

### 2. Impressions
- **What:** How many times your link appears in search results
- **Where:** Google Search Console (Performance report)
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Target:** 500+ impressions/month by month 3

### 3. Clicks
- **What:** How many people click your link from search results
- **Where:** Google Search Console (Performance report)
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Target:** 100+ clicks/month by month 3

### 4. Click-Through Rate (CTR)
- **What:** Percentage of impressions that become clicks
- **Formula:** (Clicks / Impressions) × 100
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Target:** 5-10% CTR

### 5. Traffic
- **What:** Visitors from organic search
- **Where:** Google Analytics 4
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Target:** 500+ visitors/month by month 3

### 6. Bounce Rate
- **What:** Percentage of visitors who leave without taking action
- **Where:** Google Analytics 4
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Target:** Below 50%

### 7. Average Session Duration
- **What:** How long visitors stay on your site
- **Where:** Google Analytics 4
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Target:** Above 2 minutes

### 8. Conversion Rate
- **What:** Percentage of visitors who contact you or download guide
- **Where:** Google Analytics 4 + Contact form tracking
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Target:** 5-10% conversion rate

## Red Flags & How to Fix Them

### Red Flag 1: Pages Not Indexed After 2 Weeks
**Cause:** Indexing issues or technical problems
**Fix:**
1. Check Google Search Console for errors
2. Request indexing again
3. Check for noindex tags
4. Verify sitemap is submitted
5. Check robots.txt file

### Red Flag 2: Indexed But No Impressions After 4 Weeks
**Cause:** Content quality or keyword relevance issues
**Fix:**
1. Add more content to pages (aim for 1,500+ words)
2. Improve meta descriptions
3. Add internal links
4. Check keyword relevance
5. Consider different keywords

### Red Flag 3: Impressions But Very Low CTR (<2%)
**Cause:** Poor meta descriptions or title tags
**Fix:**
1. Rewrite meta descriptions to be more compelling
2. Improve title tags
3. Add numbers or power words
4. Make it clear what the page is about

### Red Flag 4: Clicks But High Bounce Rate (>70%)
**Cause:** Page content doesn't match search intent
**Fix:**
1. Improve page content to match what people searched for
2. Add clear CTAs
3. Improve page speed
4. Make content more scannable

### Red Flag 5: No Ranking Movement After 8 Weeks
**Cause:** Not enough authority or backlinks
**Fix:**
1. Build backlinks (guest posts, press releases)
2. Add more internal links
3. Create more content
4. Improve page quality
5. Consider paid ads to boost visibility

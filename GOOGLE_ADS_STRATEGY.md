## Tracking & Optimization

### Conversion Tracking
- Form submissions → "Lead Generated"
- Phone calls → "Call Conversion"
- Contact page visits → "Page View"

### Quality Score Optimization
- **Ad Relevance:** Match ad copy to landing page content
- **Landing Page Experience:** Fast load, mobile-friendly, clear CTA
- **Click-Through Rate:** Test headlines, improve CTR

### A/B Testing Schedule
- Week 1-2: Run all ad variants, measure CTR
- Week 3-4: Pause low-performing variants
- Week 5-6: Test new headlines on winners
- Week 7-8: Optimize bids based on conversion data

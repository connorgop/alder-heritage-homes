# Local Services Ads Lead Quality Playbook

Use this while the Google Verified Local Services Ad is live. The goal is not more calls. The goal is qualified seller calls.

## Do Not Change Yet

- Do not raise budget until call quality is proven.
- Do not turn on buyer categories.
- Do not expand service area statewide.
- Do not accept Google auto-recommendations without checking whether they bring seller leads.

## Safe LSA Settings

Keep these off unless the account proves they generate seller leads:

- Buyer's agent
- First time home buyer
- Luxury properties
- New construction
- Property management

Keep or test these only if they are available and seller-focused:

- Foreclosed properties
- Other, only if calls are sellers with property addresses

Recommended schedule:

- Keep business hours if you can answer quickly.
- If calls are missed, narrow to the hours you can answer live.
- Missed calls waste the best lead source.

Recommended budget:

- Hold around the current weekly budget for 7-10 days.
- Increase only after at least 10 seller calls are reviewed.
- If more than 40% of calls are buyers, renters, agents, vendors, or unrelated, fix categories/service area before raising spend.

## Business Bio

Use this bio if Google allows edits:

```text
Alder Heritage Homes helps Fresno and Central Valley homeowners sell as-is when repairs, foreclosure, probate, inherited property, tenant issues, title/deed problems, or timing make a traditional listing difficult. Talk directly with Connor Morris, CA DRE #02219124, for a no-pressure cash offer and clear options.
```

## Lead Scoring

Log every LSA call with:

- Date
- Caller name
- Phone
- Property address
- City
- Seller or buyer
- Situation: probate, foreclosure, landlord, title/deed, repairs, taxes, divorce, vacant, other
- Motivation: cold, warm, hot
- Appointment booked
- Offer made
- Deal potential
- Bad lead reason, if any

Score calls:

- A lead: seller has address, motivation, timeline, and decision authority.
- B lead: seller has address but timeline or authority is unclear.
- C lead: researching, low motivation, no address yet.
- Bad lead: buyer, renter, vendor, job seeker, agent, wrong service, spam.

## Weekly Decision Rules

After 7-10 days:

- If A/B leads are coming in: keep budget steady or raise 15-25%.
- If calls are mostly buyers: reduce categories and service area.
- If calls are seller leads but weak: improve bio and call answering script.
- If calls are good but missed: fix answering coverage before spending more.

## Call Script

Open with:

```text
Thanks for calling Alder Heritage Homes, this is Connor. Are you calling about a property you own and may want to sell?
```

Qualify fast:

```text
What is the property address?
What is making you think about selling?
Is there a deadline or issue we need to work around?
Any mortgage, tax, probate, deed, tenant, or title issue?
If we made a fair cash offer and handled the as-is sale, when would you want to close?
```

Positioning:

```text
I can give you a no-pressure cash number, but I also want you to know the real tradeoff. A cash sale is usually about speed, certainty, and avoiding repairs. If listing would clearly net you more and you have time, I will tell you that.
```

## Search Ads Next

Local Services Ads are trust/call based. Normal Search Ads are better for exact seller intent.

First exact/phrase campaigns to launch:

- Title/deed issues
- Probate/inherited homes
- Foreclosure/behind payments
- Tired landlords
- Sell my house fast Fresno
- Sell house as-is Fresno

Never run broad match until conversion tracking proves real seller leads.

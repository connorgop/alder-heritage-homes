# Central Valley Cash Buyer Competitor Takeover Report

Date researched: 2026-05-18

## Executive Read

We can take over the smaller Central Valley markets faster than Fresno.

Fresno is crowded and expensive. Osborne Homes, Home Helpers Group, Home Assist Group, Pinnacle Home Buyers, Skyline REI, Mutari Group, and national/local cash buyer sites all compete there. The path in Fresno is trust, proof, comparison pages, and paid search discipline.

The best takeover targets are:

1. Armona
2. Reedley
3. Selma
4. Sanger
5. Hanford
6. Madera
7. Tulare
8. Visalia

Armona is the cleanest immediate win because Alder already has a strong local case study, but did not have a dedicated "sell house fast Armona" or "we buy houses Armona" landing page before this sprint.

## Strategic Positioning

Alder should not try to sound like every other "we buy houses" company.

The angle that wins:

"Licensed California real estate agent and direct cash buyer. Local Central Valley proof. We explain your options, show the math, and can often help sellers work through title, deed, probate, tax, tenant, and repair complications with escrow and the right professionals."

Avoid saying:

- "We fix any title issue"
- "Guaranteed close no matter what"
- "We stop foreclosure guaranteed"
- "We pay the most"

Say instead:

- "We can often help sellers work through title and deed issues with escrow/title professionals."
- "We buy many homes with liens, inherited ownership issues, unpaid taxes, or unclear deeds."
- "If a cash sale is not the best option, we will tell you."
- "We provide a Broker Opinion of Value so you can compare the offer against the open market."

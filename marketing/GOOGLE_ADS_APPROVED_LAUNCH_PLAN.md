# Google Ads Approved Launch Plan

Use this when the account is verified and ads are approved. The goal is not cheap clicks. The goal is motivated seller calls and form submits from Fresno and smaller Central Valley markets.

## Tracking First

Before raising budget, confirm these in Google Ads and GA4:

- Primary conversion: submitted lead form.
- Secondary conversion: phone click.
- Secondary conversion: address start.
- Final URL has UTMs on every ad.
- Lead emails include campaign/source/landing/referrer.
- Search terms are reviewed daily for the first 10 days.

Use this UTM format:

```text
?utm_source=google&utm_medium=cpc&utm_campaign={campaignid}&utm_term={keyword}&utm_content={creative}
```

## Search Console Priorities

Open the property for `https://www.alderheritagehomes.com/` and check:

1. Performance -> Search results -> Queries:
   - Find impressions with low CTR.
   - Rewrite titles/meta for pages with impressions but weak clicks.
2. Pages:
   - Find pages with impressions but no clicks.
   - Add stronger above-the-fold copy and internal links.
3. Indexing -> Pages:
   - Submit the new title/deed/probate/foreclosure pages.
   - Inspect URL for the highest-priority landing pages after each deploy.
4. Links:
   - Track which pages earn internal links.
   - Add internal links from hubs to money pages with exact natural anchors.

## Budget Rules

- Start small until real conversions record correctly.
- Do not use broad match in week 1.
- Do not let Google auto-apply recommendations without review.
- Increase budget only on campaigns with actual form/call leads.
- If a campaign gets clicks but no calls/forms after 25-40 clicks, inspect search terms and landing-page match before spending more.

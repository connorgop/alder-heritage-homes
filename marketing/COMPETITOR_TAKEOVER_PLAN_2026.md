# Alder Heritage Homes Competitor Takeover Plan

Last updated: 2026-05-20

## Goal

Win more motivated seller leads in Fresno and Central Valley by owning three high-intent lanes:

1. Probate and inherited homes.
2. Foreclosure and behind-on-payment sellers.
3. Tired landlords with tenant, repair, and portfolio problems.

## What Competitors Are Doing

Osborne Homes:
- Strongest proof stack: long operating history, broad California footprint, thousands of homes claimed, reviews, no-hassle guarantee.
- Main message: as-is, fast close, no pressure, no fees, close in as few as 7 days.
- Vulnerability: broad California positioning makes smaller-city and legal/title-specific pages easier to out-localize.

Home Assist / Home Helpers:
- Strong SEO focus on Fresno and Central Valley cash buyer searches.
- Main message: cash offer, no repairs, no realtor commission, close quickly.
- Vulnerability: many pages sound generic. Alder can beat them with better proof, licensed-agent clarity, and deeper problem-specific content.

Pinnacle:
- Main message: family-owned, legitimate buyer, direct buyer, problem-solver.
- Vulnerability: thinner content footprint and fewer city/problem landing pages.

Skyline REI:
- Strongest local proof angle: renovated homes, direct end-buyer, offer match positioning, visible sold properties.
- Vulnerability: brand overlaps with Alder/Connor but can confuse sellers. Alder should clearly separate the seller-help brand and use proof carefully.

Foglio:
- Simple local positioning: Fresno, third-generation, all cash, as-is.
- Vulnerability: very thin content. Easy to outrank with useful pages and case studies.

## Winning Position

Alder should not just say "we buy houses fast." Everyone says that.

Own this message:

"A licensed local direct buyer for complicated Central Valley homes: probate, foreclosure, title/deed issues, tenant problems, unpaid taxes, and rentals that normal buyers cannot close on."

4. Call source is captured if possible.
5. CRM or spreadsheet logs: page, keyword/campaign if available, seller situation, city, phone, timestamp.

## Follow-Up Machine

Every lead should get:

1. Immediate call within 5 minutes.
2. Text if no answer:
   "Hi, this is Connor with Alder Heritage Homes. I got your request about the property. What is the best time to talk for 5 minutes?"
3. Same-day second call.
4. Next-day text with one helpful question:
   - Probate: "Do you already have Letters from the court or is probate still being opened?"
   - Foreclosure: "Have you received a Notice of Trustee Sale yet, or only a Notice of Default?"
   - Landlord: "Is the tenant current, behind, or is the property vacant?"
5. Add to 14-day follow-up sequence.

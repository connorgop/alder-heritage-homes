# Search Term Negative Keyword Playbook

Review Google Ads search terms at least twice per week.

## Kill These Immediately

Add as negatives when they appear:

- Jobs, careers, salary, hiring, internship.
- Courses, training, coaching, webinar, seminar.
- Realtor, real estate agent, broker license.
- Zillow, Redfin, Realtor.com, MLS.
- Rentals, apartments, Section 8, lease, rooms for rent.
- Templates, PDF, sample contract, forms.
- Property tax payment, assessor, parcel map, public records.
- Land for sale, commercial property, office space.
- Definition, meaning, how to become.

## Watch Carefully

These can be good or bad depending on the phrase:

- Probate
- Tax lien
- Title issue
- Foreclosure
- Code violation
- Inherited house
- Eviction

Keep if the query clearly shows a homeowner trying to sell. Negate if it is legal research, government payment, landlord forms, or job intent.

## Good Search Terms to Promote

Move these into exact match when they convert:

- sell my house fast fresno
- cash home buyer fresno
- sell inherited house fresno
- sell house with title issues
- sell house with unpaid property taxes
- sell house with multiple heirs
- sell house before foreclosure auction
- sell house as is fresno

## Budget Rule

Do not increase a campaign budget if:

- Search terms have not been reviewed in 72 hours.
- There are no verified form or call conversions.
- More than 30% of spend is going to research/rental/job queries.

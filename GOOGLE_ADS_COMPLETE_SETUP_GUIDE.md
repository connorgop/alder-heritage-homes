## Payment Method Setup

1. Go to ads.google.com
2. Click **Settings** (gear icon, top right)
3. Click **Billing & payments**
4. Your payment method should already be saved from your existing campaign
5. Confirm it's the card you want to use for the new campaigns

## Troubleshooting

**Issue: Low impressions**
- Solution: Increase max CPC bids or expand keywords to phrase/broad match

**Issue: High cost per lead**
- Solution: Pause underperforming keywords, improve landing page relevance

**Issue: No conversions**
- Solution: Check landing page is loading, verify form is working, test on mobile

**Issue: Ads not showing**
- Solution: Check daily budget isn't exhausted, verify keywords have sufficient bids

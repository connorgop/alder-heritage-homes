# Google Business Profile (GBP) Weekly Post Strategy

## Overview
Google Business Profile (GBP) posts are a direct local ranking signal. Posting weekly updates about deals, services, and company news can improve your local search visibility by 15-30%.

**Goal:** Post 1-2 times per week to maintain engagement and signal activity to Google.

---

## Why GBP Posts Matter

1. **Local Ranking Signal** - Google rewards active, updated profiles
2. **Fresh Content** - Shows your business is active and current
3. **Engagement** - Drives clicks, calls, and website visits
4. **Trust Building** - Customers see recent activity and testimonials
5. **Competitive Advantage** - Most competitors don't post regularly

## Post Optimization Tips

### 1. Use Emojis Strategically
- 🏠 House/Real Estate
- ⭐ Reviews/Testimonials
- 📚 Educational
- 🎯 Promotions
- ✓ Checkmarks for benefits
- 💰 Money/Deals

### 2. Keep It Short
- **Ideal length:** 150-250 characters
- **Reason:** Mobile-friendly, easier to read
- **CTA:** Always include phone number or link

### 3. Include Keywords
- City names (Fresno, Clovis, Visalia, etc.)
- Situation keywords (foreclosure, probate, inherited, etc.)
- Service keywords (cash buyer, sell fast, etc.)

### 4. Link to Relevant Pages
- Deal Showcase → Case Study page
- Educational → Blog post or service page
- Testimonial → Reviews page
- Promotion → Calculator or offer page

### 5. Encourage Engagement
- Ask questions: "Inherited a home? We can help."
- Include CTAs: "Call (559) 281-8016"
- Link to reviews: "Leave us a review"

---

## Posting Best Practices

### Timing
- **Best days:** Monday-Thursday (avoid weekends)
- **Best times:** 9 AM - 12 PM or 5 PM - 7 PM
- **Frequency:** 1-2 posts per week (consistent)

### Content Mix
- **50% Deal Showcases** - Social proof, urgency
- **30% Educational** - Authority, keywords
- **15% Testimonials** - Trust, social proof
- **5% Promotions** - CTAs, lead generation

### Monitoring
- Check engagement daily
- Respond to comments/questions within 24 hours
- Track which posts get most engagement
- Replicate high-performing content

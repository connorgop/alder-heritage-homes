# Lead Magnet Strategy: "5 Critical Mistakes to Avoid When Selling Your House for Cash"

## Overview

The PDF guide "5 Critical Mistakes to Avoid When Selling Your House for Cash" is a powerful lead magnet designed to capture email addresses from high-intent sellers who are considering selling their homes for cash.

**Lead Magnet Details:**
- **Title:** 5 Critical Mistakes to Avoid When Selling Your House for Cash
- **Format:** PDF (8 pages, 172 KB)
- **Landing Page:** `/lead-magnet` or `/free-guide`
- **Form Fields:** Name, Email, Phone, City (2-step form)
- **Value Proposition:** Exposes wholesaler tactics and teaches sellers how to protect themselves

---

## Why This Lead Magnet Works

### 1. Addresses a Real Pain Point
- 99% of sellers don't know the difference between wholesalers and direct buyers
- They're scared of getting trapped or losing money
- This guide gives them the knowledge to protect themselves

### 2. Positions You as the Expert
- You understand the wholesaler problem (because you worked there)
- You know all the red flags
- You offer the solution (direct buyer alternative)

### 3. High-Intent Audience
- People downloading this guide are actively considering selling for cash
- They're worried about wholesalers
- They're ready to make a decision

### 4. Builds Trust Before the Pitch
- The guide is educational, not salesy
- It teaches them what to look for
- It positions you as the honest alternative

### 5. Captures Qualified Leads
- You get their email, phone, and city
- You know they're interested in cash sales
- You can follow up with targeted offers

## Lead Magnet Optimization

### A/B Testing Ideas

**Test 1: Form Fields**
- Variant A: Name, Email, Phone, City (current)
- Variant B: Name, Email only (shorter form)
- Variant C: Name, Email, Phone (no city)
- **Metric:** Conversion rate, lead quality

**Test 2: Headline**
- Variant A: "5 Critical Mistakes to Avoid When Selling Your House for Cash"
- Variant B: "WARNING: 99% of Cash Buyers Are Wholesalers"
- Variant C: "How to Protect Yourself From Wholesaler Scams"
- **Metric:** CTR, conversion rate

**Test 3: CTA Button Text**
- Variant A: "Download My Free Guide"
- Variant B: "Get the Free PDF"
- Variant C: "Yes, Send Me the Guide"
- **Metric:** Click rate, conversion rate

**Test 4: Form Steps**
- Variant A: 2-step form (current)
- Variant B: 1-step form (all fields at once)
- Variant C: 3-step form (add more info)
- **Metric:** Completion rate, lead quality

**Test 5: Value Proposition**
- Variant A: "Learn the 5 mistakes wholesalers don't want you to know"
- Variant B: "Protect yourself from losing $10K-$50K to wholesalers"
- Variant C: "Get the insider knowledge that saved 1000+ homeowners"
- **Metric:** CTR, conversion rate

### Optimization Priorities

1. **Conversion Rate:** If conversion rate < 15%, improve form or value prop
2. **Cost Per Lead:** If CPL > $5, improve targeting or creative
3. **Lead Quality:** If conversion rate (lead to customer) < 3%, improve messaging
4. **Volume:** If leads < 50/week, increase budget or expand channels

## Integration with Sales Process

### Lead Scoring

**High Priority (Score 80+):**
- Downloaded guide + filled out form + called within 24 hours
- Downloaded guide + filled out form + city = Fresno or Clovis
- Downloaded guide + filled out form + phone provided

**Medium Priority (Score 50-79):**
- Downloaded guide + filled out form + city = Visalia or Bakersfield
- Downloaded guide + filled out form + no phone provided
- Downloaded guide + clicked email link

**Low Priority (Score < 50):**
- Downloaded guide + no follow-up
- Downloaded guide + unsubscribed from email
- Downloaded guide + marked as spam

### Sales Follow-Up

**High Priority Leads:**
- Call within 1 hour
- Send cash offer within 24 hours
- Mention price match guarantee

**Medium Priority Leads:**
- Call within 4 hours
- Send email with cash offer within 24 hours
- Include testimonials and case studies

**Low Priority Leads:**
- Add to nurture email sequence
- Send monthly market updates
- Re-engage with new content

# Google Ads Launch Guide - When Rankings Hit Top 20

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT: DO NOT LAUNCH ADS YET

**Wait until your pages are ranking in the top 20 for target keywords.** This will happen around **Week 8-12** after submitting to Google Search Console.

**Timeline:**
- Week 1-2: Pages indexed
- Week 4-8: Positions 20-50
- **Week 8-12: Positions 10-20 ← LAUNCH ADS HERE**
- Month 3+: Top 10 rankings

---

## When to Launch Google Ads

### Launch Signals (All must be true):
- [ ] Pages are indexed in Google Search Console
- [ ] Average position is below 20 for target keywords
- [ ] You have 200+ impressions/week
- [ ] You have 20+ clicks/week
- [ ] You understand what keywords are working

### Check Your Rankings:
1. Go to Google Search Console
2. Click "Performance"
3. Look at "Average Position" for your target keywords
4. If average position is below 20, you're ready

## Bidding Strategy

### Maximize Conversions (Recommended)
- Google automatically sets bids to maximize conversions
- Works best when you have conversion tracking set up
- Requires at least 30 conversions in past 30 days
- **Best for:** Scaling campaigns

### Target CPA (Cost Per Action)
- You set a target cost per conversion
- Google tries to hit that target
- **Target CPA:** $25-50 per lead
- **Best for:** Controlling costs

### Manual CPC (Cost Per Click)
- You set max bid per click
- More control but requires more management
- **Max CPC:** $3-15 depending on keyword
- **Best for:** Starting out

**Recommendation:** Start with Manual CPC, switch to Maximize Conversions after 30 days.

## Conversion Tracking Setup

### Step 1: Create Conversion Actions
1. Go to Google Ads
2. Click "Tools & Settings" → "Conversions"
3. Click "New Conversion Action"
4. Select "Website"
5. Enter your website URL
6. Choose conversion type:
   - **Phone calls:** Track calls to (559) 281-8016
   - **Form submissions:** Track contact form submissions
   - **Page views:** Track visits to /lead-magnet

### Step 2: Add Tracking Code
1. Google will give you a conversion tracking code
2. Add it to your website
3. Test it to make sure it works

### Step 3: Monitor Conversions
1. Go to Google Ads
2. Click "Campaigns"
3. Look at "Conversions" column
4. Track cost per conversion

**Target:** $25-50 cost per lead

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Mistake 1: Launching Before Ranking
**Problem:** Ads are expensive when you're not ranking organically
**Solution:** Wait until top 20 rankings (Week 8-12)

### ❌ Mistake 2: Wrong Landing Pages
**Problem:** Sending branded keyword traffic to homepage
**Solution:** Use `/ads/competitor-warning` for branded keywords

### ❌ Mistake 3: No Conversion Tracking
**Problem:** Can't measure ROI or optimize
**Solution:** Set up conversion tracking before launching

### ❌ Mistake 4: Bidding Too High
**Problem:** Wasting budget on expensive clicks
**Solution:** Start with $3-5 CPC, increase gradually

### ❌ Mistake 5: Not Pausing Losers
**Problem:** Continuing to spend on non-converting keywords
**Solution:** Review weekly and pause low-performers

### Scale When:
- [ ] CPA is below $50

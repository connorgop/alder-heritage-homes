# Google Ads Custom Intent Audiences Setup Guide

## Create 3 High-Value Audiences for Alder Heritage Homes

This guide will walk you through creating 3 custom intent audiences that target people actively searching for probate solutions, home selling, and estate planning. **Total setup time: 10-15 minutes.**

---

## What Are Custom Intent Audiences?

Custom intent audiences target people who are actively searching for specific keywords and topics on Google. These are **high-intent users** who are ready to take action.

**Why they work:**
- People are actively searching for your services
- 5-10x higher conversion rates than regular audiences
- Lower cost per conversion
- Perfect for cash home buyers

---

## Part 1: Create Audience #1 - "Sell My House Fast"

### Step 1: Go to Google Ads Audiences

1. Open Google Ads: https://ads.google.com
2. Click **Tools & settings** (wrench icon) in top right
3. Under "Shared library," click **Audiences**
4. Click the blue **Create audience** button

### Step 2: Select Custom Intent Audience

1. You'll see a list of audience types
2. Look for **"Custom intent"** (not "Website visitors")
3. Click on **Custom intent**

### Step 3: Name Your Audience

**Audience name:** `Sell My House Fast - High Intent`

### Step 4: Add Keywords

In the "Keywords" section, add these search terms (people searching for these are your ideal customers):

```
sell my house fast
sell my house quickly
sell my house for cash
sell my house as is
need to sell my house fast
how to sell my house fast
sell my house without realtor
sell my house to cash buyer
sell my house in fresno
sell my house quickly fresno
fastest way to sell house
sell house fast no repairs
sell house for cash fresno
sell my house no inspection
```

**How to add keywords:**
1. Click in the keywords box
2. Type each keyword and press Enter
3. Continue until all keywords are added

### Step 5: Add Topics (Optional but Recommended)

Scroll down to "Topics" section and add:
- Home selling
- Real estate
- Property sales
- Home buyers

### Step 6: Set Membership Duration

- **Membership duration:** 30 days
- This means people stay in the audience for 30 days after their last search

### Step 7: Save the Audience

Click the blue **Create audience** button at the bottom

**Result:** Audience #1 is now created and will start collecting people who search for these terms.

## Part 4: Verify All 3 Audiences Are Created

### Step 1: Check Audiences List

1. Go to **Tools & settings > Audiences**
2. You should see 3 new audiences:
   - ✓ Sell My House Fast - High Intent
   - ✓ Probate & Inheritance - High Intent
   - ✓ Trusts & Wills - High Intent

### Step 2: Check Audience Size

Each audience should show:
- **Audience size:** "Eligible" (means it's building)
- **Status:** "Active"

**Note:** It takes 24-48 hours for audiences to start building. Don't worry if you see "Eligible" instead of a number - that's normal.

## Part 5: Add Audiences to Your Campaigns

Now that your audiences are created, you need to add them to your campaigns so they start getting ads.

### Step 5.1: Add to "Highest Paying $ Home Buyer" Campaign

1. Go to **Campaigns**
2. Click on **"Highest Paying $ Home Buyer"**
3. Click on **"Ad groups"** tab
4. Click on your ad group
5. Click **"Audiences"** section
6. Click **"+ Add audience"**
7. Select **"Sell My House Fast - High Intent"**
8. Set bid adjustment: **+20%** (bid higher for this valuable audience)
9. Click **Save**

**Repeat for other audiences:**
- Add "Probate & Inheritance - High Intent" with +25% bid
- Add "Trusts & Wills - High Intent" with +15% bid

### Step 5.2: Verify Audiences Are Active

1. Go back to your ad group
2. Under "Audiences," you should see all 3 audiences listed
3. Status should show **"Active"**

## Monitoring Your Audiences

### Weekly Checklist

Every Monday, check:

1. **Audience size** - Is it growing?
   - Go to Tools & settings > Audiences
   - Check each audience size
   - Should grow by 10-20% per week

2. **Campaign performance** - Are you getting conversions?
   - Go to Campaigns > Ad groups > Audiences
   - Check impressions, clicks, conversions
   - Look for high-performing audiences

3. **Bid adjustments** - Are they optimal?
   - If audience has high conversion rate, increase bid by +10-20%
   - If audience has low conversion rate, decrease bid by -10-20%

### Monthly Optimization

**Week 1-2 of month:**
- [ ] Check audience sizes (should be 2-3x larger than last month)
- [ ] Analyze conversion data
- [ ] Identify top-performing audiences

**Week 3-4 of month:**
- [ ] Increase bids for top performers by +10-20%
- [ ] Decrease bids for underperformers by -10-20%
- [ ] Consider creating new audiences based on performance

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Audience shows "Eligible" but no size number

**Solution:** This is normal. Audiences take 24-48 hours to build. Check back tomorrow.

### Problem: Audience size is very small (< 100)

**Solution:** 
1. Add more keywords to the audience
2. Increase membership duration (30 → 60 days)
3. Wait 1-2 weeks for audience to grow

### Problem: No impressions from audience after 2 weeks

**Solution:**
1. Check bid adjustment (should be +10-30%)
2. Check if audience is added to ad group
3. Verify campaign is active and has budget
4. Check if keywords are too niche

### Problem: High cost per conversion from audience

**Solution:**
1. Lower bid adjustment by -10-20%
2. Improve landing page (reduce form fields)
3. Test new ad copy
4. Consider removing audience if CPA stays high

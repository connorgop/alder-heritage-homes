# Central Valley Price Comparison Strategy

## Overview
Create city-specific price comparison pages that show sellers:
1. **Average home prices** in their city
2. **Cash vs. listing comparison** (what they'll net)
3. **Market trends** (appreciation, days on market)
4. **Why Alder Heritage pays more** (direct buyer, no wholesaler fees)

---

## Cities to Target (Priority Order)

### Tier 1: High Priority (Build First)
1. **Fresno** - Largest market, most searches
2. **Clovis** - Affluent suburb, higher prices
3. **Visalia** - Strong market, 2nd largest city
4. **Bakersfield** - Large market, high volume

### Tier 2: Medium Priority (Build Second)

15. **Reedley** - Small market

## Page Structure for Each City

### URL Pattern
- `/price-comparison-fresno`
- `/price-comparison-clovis`
- `/price-comparison-visalia`
- etc.

### Content Sections

#### 1. Hero Section
- City name & average home price
- "See what your home is worth in cash"
- CTA: "Get Your Cash Offer"

#### 2. Market Overview
- Average home price (last 12 months)
- Price trend (up/down/stable)
- Days on market (average)
- Homes sold (last 30 days)
- Cash sale percentage

#### 3. Price Ranges by Neighborhood
| Neighborhood | Avg Price | Cash Buyers Active | Days on Market |
|--------------|-----------|-------------------|----------------|
| Neighborhood 1 | $XXX,XXX | High/Medium/Low | XX days |
| Neighborhood 2 | $XXX,XXX | High/Medium/Low | XX days |
| etc. | | | |

#### 4. Cash vs. Listing Comparison
**Example for $300,000 home:**

| Factor | Cash Sale | Traditional Listing |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Sale Price | $300,000 | $310,000 |
| Real Estate Commission (6%) | $0 | $18,600 |
| Closing Costs (2-3%) | $0 | $6,200 |
| Repairs Needed | $0 | $5,000 |
| Days on Market | 3 | 45 |
| **NET TO YOU** | **$300,000** | **$280,200** |

**Key Insight:** "Even though the listing price is higher, you net MORE with a cash sale because there are no fees, no repairs, and no contingencies."

- Direct buyer (no wholesaler middleman)

- No assignment contracts

- "See what we paid for homes like yours"

#### 7. Testimonials from [City]

- "Real people, real results"

#### 8. FAQ
